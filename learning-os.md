---

Example:

## Step 2 — Create Your Own Mental Model

Ask:

* What problem exists?
* Why do naive systems fail?
* What changes in the architecture?
* What tradeoff appears?

This is the retention layer.

---

One of:

* LinkedIn post
* diagram
* notes
* architecture sketch
* failure analysis
* comparison table

This forces compression.

Compression creates memory.

After 2–3 weeks:

* reread your own explanation,
* improve it,
* connect it to another concept.

This is how deep understanding forms.
